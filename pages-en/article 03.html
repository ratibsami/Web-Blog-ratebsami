The game INDIKA offers remarkable religious perspectives alongside exceptional graphics

Wednesday, March 14, 2024

|
It's a third-person, story-driven, open-world game set in Russia at the end of the 19th century, where religious perspectives clash with harsh realities. INDIKA tells the story of a young nun who, accompanied by her extraordinary companion, her own demon, embarks on a journey of self-discovery.
