About Me

Enter the World of Ratib Sami: UI/UX Designer, Writer, and Passionate Existentialist Welcome to a world filled with enthusiasm and eagerness! Here, I, Ratib Sami, born in 2006, proudly embrace the roles of a UI/UX designer, programmer, and honorary writer for the Persian Wikipedia, reveling in the beauties and depths of the geek world. I have a penchant for the pioneering novels of the existentialist movement, such as those by Fyodor Dostoevsky, Albert Camus, and Paul Sartre. In these tales, not only do they depict the path of human life, but they also draw me into deeper rationality and mentality due to the depth and questions they raise. In this blog, we share our knowledge and experiences with each other. From expertise in UI design to passionate embraces of the geek world, everything is here. So, join me, and let's embark on this spiritual and mental journey in the geek world together.
